# Energy & Power Data Sources

## Government / Regulatory

| Source | URL | What It Provides |
|--------|-----|-----------------|
| **EIA (Form 860)** | https://www.eia.gov/electricity/data/eia860/ | Generator inventory — capacity, fuel type, location, ownership |
| **EIA (Form 861)** | https://www.eia.gov/electricity/data/eia861/ | Utility-level sales, revenue, customer counts |
| **EIA (Form 923)** | https://www.eia.gov/electricity/data/eia923/ | Monthly generation, fuel consumption, receipts |
| **EIA Annual Energy Outlook** | https://www.eia.gov/outlooks/aeo/ | Long-range energy supply/demand forecasts |
| **FERC (Form 714)** | https://www.ferc.gov/industries-data/electric/general-information/electric-industry-forms/form-no-714-annual-electric | Hourly load data by balancing authority |
| **FERC eLibrary** | https://elibrary.ferc.gov/ | Regulatory filings, rate cases, interconnection disputes |
| **EPA eGRID** | https://www.epa.gov/egrid | Emissions factors by grid region and plant |
| **EPA GHGRP** | https://www.epa.gov/ghgreporting | Facility-level greenhouse gas emissions reporting |
| **DOE (LBNL)** | https://emp.lbl.gov/publications | Utility-scale solar, wind, storage cost and performance data |

## Industry Organizations

| Source | URL | What It Provides |
|--------|-----|-----------------|
| **The Green Grid** | https://www.thegreengrid.org/ | PUE, WUE, CUE methodology and benchmarks |
| **ASHRAE TC 9.9** | https://tc0909.ashraetcs.org/ | Thermal guidelines for datacom equipment |
| **Uptime Institute** | https://uptimeinstitute.com/ | Tier classifications, annual survey, PUE benchmarks |
| **Open Compute Project** | https://www.opencompute.org/ | Open datacenter hardware designs, power/cooling specs |
