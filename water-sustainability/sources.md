# Water & Sustainability Data Sources

## Water Risk & Mapping

| Source | URL | What It Provides |
|--------|-----|-----------------|
| **WRI Aqueduct** | https://www.wri.org/aqueduct | Global water stress maps, risk projections, basin-level data |
| **USGS Water Data** | https://waterdata.usgs.gov/ | Real-time streamflow, groundwater levels, water use statistics |
| **NASA GRACE-FO** | https://gracefo.jpl.nasa.gov/ | Satellite-based groundwater and surface water change detection |
| **NOAA Drought Monitor** | https://droughtmonitor.unl.edu/ | Weekly US drought severity mapping |

## Efficiency Standards & Benchmarks

| Source | URL | What It Provides |
|--------|-----|-----------------|
| **The Green Grid** | https://www.thegreengrid.org/ | PUE, WUE, CUE definitions, measurement protocols, white papers |
| **ASHRAE TC 9.9** | https://tc0909.ashraetcs.org/ | Thermal guidelines for datacom equipment, allowable temperature ranges |
| **Uptime Institute** | https://uptimeinstitute.com/ | Annual global PUE survey, Tier standards, resiliency assessments |
| **DOE Better Buildings** | https://betterbuildingssolutioncenter.energy.gov/ | Energy efficiency case studies, federal program data |
